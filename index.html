What do we do?

We create Terrariums: glass containers with a garden inside, that can thrive inside your home. A houseplant, but without the mess, effort or commitment. These products are both cheap, and require little to no involvement after you buy them. In fact, terrariums are shown to boost the mental health of their owners!

Who Are We?

We are The Jardin: A youth enterprise company with an eye for home decoration. We create and cultivate handmade terrariums for Amersham and the surrounding area.We are a group of hardworking individuals and are passionate about creating a product that you can both appreciate and enjoy, and will make a great addition to your home.
