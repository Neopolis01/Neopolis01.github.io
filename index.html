Who We Are

We are The Jardin: A young enterprise company with an eye for home decoration. What We Do

We create Terrariums: glass containers with a garden inside, that can thrive inside your home. A houseplant, but without the mess, effort or commitment. These products require little to no involvement after you buy them, and are shown to boost the mental health of their owners!
